# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)

## ✅ Do's
- [ ] Think before you post — consider long-term impact on your reputation  
- [ ] Maintain a professional online presence (especially on LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.)  
- [ ] Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation  
- [ ] Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- [ ] Respect others' opinions and engage in constructive discussions  


## ❌ Don'ts
- [ ] Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- [ ] Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or harassment  
- [ ] Don’t post content under emotional impulse (anger, frustration, etc.)  
- [ ] Don’t plagiarize or use others’ content without credit  
- [ ] Don’t overshare details about your daily routine or location
