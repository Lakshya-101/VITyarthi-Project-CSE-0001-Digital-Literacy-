# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)

## ✅ Prevention Tips
- [ ] Use strong, unique passwords for each account (preferably with a password manager)  
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps  
- [ ] Verify links before clicking — especially in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS messages  
- [ ] Keep your devices and apps updated to patch security vulnerabilities  
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities (banking, logins); use a VPN if necessary  
- [ ] Review privacy settings on platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook regularly  
- [ ] Do not download pirated software, cracked apps, or unknown attachments  
- [ ] Be cautious of job/internship scams asking for upfront fees or personal details  

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety (Critical)
- [ ] Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials with anyone — banks never ask for this  
- [ ] Always verify “Collect Requests” in UPI apps before approving — receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN  
- [ ] Double-check QR codes and payment IDs before sending money  
- [ ] Avoid screen-sharing apps when accessing banking or payment apps  

## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong
- [ ] Immediately report fraud at the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- [ ] Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** for urgent financial fraud assistance  
- [ ] Inform your bank and block your card/account if unauthorized transactions occur  
- [ ] Preserve evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, messages) for investigation
